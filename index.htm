Key considerations
    Coverage

    Built for five kinds of conflict

    Different games reward different instincts. Adish's advice bank is tuned per domain, not a single generic script.

    Chess

    Opening plans, middlegame imbalances, and endgame technique.

    Poker

    Position, stack depth, and range-based betting decisions.

    MOBA / RTS

    Lane pressure, objective timing, and macro decision-making.

    Board games

    Resource tempo, tableau building, and endgame scoring pushes.

    Negotiation

    Leverage reads, concession pacing, and BATNA-aware framing.

    Under the hood

    What the read actually weighs

    Position modeling

    Reads your description for who's ahead, who's behind, and how far apart the two sides really are.

    Risk calibration

    Turns your stated aggression and current standing into a single, honest risk read — not just "attack" or "fold."

    Counter-strategy detection

    Flags the phase-specific traps and reversals that tend to catch players at exactly this stage of the game.

    Domain-tuned voice

    Chess advice reads like chess advice. Poker advice reads like poker advice. No one-size-fits-all script.
